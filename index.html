Tone perception test

This form supports research into how the human brain perceives different noise frequencies while under the influence of psychoactive substances, such as cannabis, psilocybin, LSD, and related compounds.

For the most reliable results, we recommend using a high-quality audio system. Click each tone button as many times as needed. If you perceive a single continuous tone, select the checkbox. If you perceive a change in the tone midway, leave the checkbox unselected.
